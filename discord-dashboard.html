Top Narratives

  • Covered-call execution dominates, but the crowd is split between weeklies with 85-95% capture and 30-45 DTE structures with 50% auto-buybacks.
  • Strike selection is increasingly TA-driven rather than purely delta-driven: resistance, mean reversion, and trend are the recurring filters.
  • IBIT has emerged as the preferred crypto-linked income vehicle over FBTC because the chain is cleaner, strikes are denser, and rolling is easier.
  • TSLA remains the emotional center of the group: everyone wants the premium, but many have learned not to sell calls at bottoms or into violent upside reversals.
  • Risk management is the real edge: laddering strikes, accepting assignment when profitable, and avoiding panic rolls are the repeated lessons.

Bottom Line: The current Discord flow is overwhelmingly about monetizing volatility through covered calls, with TSLA as the anchor ticker and IBIT as the cleanest repeatable income setup. ALAB and MRVL stand out as case studies in active management and credit rolling, while MSTR remains the high-octane premium engine that can punish poor timing. The strongest shared edge is process: sell into resistance, know your assignment tolerance, ladder your exposure, and let time decay do the work.
